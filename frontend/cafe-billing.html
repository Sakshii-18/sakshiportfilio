Project Overview

What the project is

The Cafe Billing Management System is a business application for cafes and small food businesses. It helps manage menu items, customer orders, GST calculation, invoices and sales reports.

The Problem

Manual billing systems often lead to calculation mistakes and inefficient order handling.

Billing by hand can slow service, create tax errors and make sales tracking difficult.

The Solution

Automated billing and sales workflow.

Created a digital billing solution that automates order processing, tax calculation, invoice generation and business reporting.
